Business Development Officer

Abu Dhabi, UAE Full Time

Responsibilities

  • Identifying potential clients and building relationships with them through networking, cold calling, and other means.
  • Analyzing market trends and competitor activities to identify opportunities for growth and improvement.
  • Creating and implementing strategic plans to increase sales, revenue, and profitability.
  • Developing proposals and presentations for new business opportunities and pitching them to potential clients.
  • Negotiating contracts and agreements with clients to ensure mutually beneficial relationships.

Qualifications

  • A Bachelor's degree in business administration, marketing, finance, or a related field is typically required. An MBA or other advanced degree may be preferred.
  • A minimum of 0-2 years of experience in business development, sales, or marketing is typically required. Experience in the relevant industry or market is also preferred.
  • Excellent communication skills, both written and verbal, are necessary to effectively communicate with clients, team members, and stakeholders.
  • The ability to adapt to changing market conditions and industry trends is essential for success in this role.
  • A BDO must be able to work effectively with cross-functional teams and collaborate with internal departments to achieve company goals.

Telesales Executive

Abu Dhabi, UAE Full Time

Responsibilities

  • Researching and identifying potential customers through various sources such as online directories, social media platforms, and cold-calling.
  • Making outbound calls to potential customers to introduce the company's products or services, and to generate interest and leads.
  • Qualifying leads by understanding the customer's needs, budget, and decision-making process.
  • Providing information about the products or services, answering questions, and addressing concerns.
  • Meeting or exceeding sales targets set by the company and contributing to the overall revenue of the business.

Qualifications

  • A high school diploma or equivalent is usually required, and a bachelor's degree in business, marketing, or a related field may be preferred.
  • Excellent communication skills, both verbal and written, are necessary to effectively communicate with customers and team members.
  • Strong persuasion skills are essential to convince potential customers to make a purchase or sign a contract.
  • Strong customer service skills are necessary to build rapport with customers and address their concerns.
  • Basic computer skills are required to enter and maintain customer information in a database, prepare reports, and use various software applications.
